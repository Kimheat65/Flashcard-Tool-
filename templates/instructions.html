How to Use the Flashcard Quiz

Welcome! Here’s how the quiz works:

  • Click Start Quiz to begin.
  • Read the question and type your answer in the box.
  • Your score will update automatically after each question.
  • When you finish, you’ll see your total score.
  • You can upload custom questions as a .txt file on the homepage.
  • You can also add individual questions manually.
